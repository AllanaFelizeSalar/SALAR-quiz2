HOW I BECOME MYSELF


Elementary Journey

Time flies by so quickly. Thinking back, my elementary journey was like a movie, with a lot of things happening. Elementary is six years of growing up and is presumably a period of your life where you explore and make a lot of peers.

Highschool Journey

Everything begins in highschool; your transfomation from a socially disorganized kid to a self - assured teen throughout this phase of your education is brimming with exhilarating opportunities. High school is typically where lifetime reputations and images are formed.

Senior Highschool Journey

Being a senior high school student implies having the ability to deal with and overcome the quintessential difficulties and barriers that a typical student would experience. Senior high taught me a lot of lessons. These lessons helped me shape the person I am today.
